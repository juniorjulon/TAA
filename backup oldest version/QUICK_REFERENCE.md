## The 4 Pillars Explained

### **🏛️ PILLAR 1: FUNDAMENTALS (Weight: 25%)**

| What | When Works | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Measures**: Economic growth direction, credit health | **Best Horizon**: Cycle turning points (3–12M) | **Bullish** (z > +0.5): PMI >50, earnings up, leverage healthy | **👍 Overweight cyclicals, EM, equities** |
| **Key Data**: PMI, earnings revisions, default rates | **Fails**: Mid-cycle, QE era | **Bearish** (z < −0.5): PMI <50, earnings down, defaults rising | **👎 Raise cash, favor bonds/gold** |

---

### **📈 PILLAR 2: MOMENTUM & SENTIMENT (Weight: 30%)**

| What | When Works | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Measures**: Price trends, market fear/greed | **Best Horizon**: 3–12M (sweet spot for momentum) | **Bullish** (z > +0.5): RSI 50–70, MA50>MA200, 12M ret >+15% | **👍 Ride the trend; lean into risk** |
| **Key Data**: 12M returns, RSI(14), MA50/200, MOVE | **Fails**: Regime shifts, bottoms/tops | **Bearish** (z < −0.5): RSI <30, MA50<MA200, negative returns | **👎 Defensive; reduce volatility** |

---

### **💧 PILLAR 3: POSITIONING (Weight: 15%)**

| What | When Works | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Measures**: Fund flows, spreads, supply/demand | **Best Horizon**: Sentiment extremes (3–12M contrarian) | **Bullish** (z > +0.5): Spreads tightening, inflows, low issuance | **👍 Risk-on; overweight risky assets** |
| **Key Data**: OAS vs. MA, EPFR flows, issuance, margin debt | **Fails**: Long-duration (noisy, contaminated by hedging) | **Bearish** (z < −0.5): Spreads widening, outflows, heavy supply | **👎 Risk-off; reduce equity exposure** |

---

### **💰 PILLAR 4: VALUATION (Weight: 20%)**

| What | When Works | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Measures**: Absolute/relative valuations, real yields | **Best Horizon**: 3–12M, excellent for 1–3Y | **Bullish** (z > +0.5): P/E <16x, CAPE bottom 25%, real yield >2% | **👍 Build positions; safety margin** |
| **Key Data**: P/E, CAPE, 10Y TIPS yield, ERP | **Fails**: Short term (<3M, R²≈10%) | **Bearish** (z < −0.5): P/E >22x, CAPE top 25%, ZIRP | **👎 Trim risk; valuation froth** |

## Pillar Strength Zones (Radar Chart Guide)

```
                    BULLISH
                      ▲
                      │
         VALUATION    │    MOMENTUM
         (Fair/Cheap) │ (Strong/Persistent)
                      │
    POSITIONING ◄─────┼─────► FUNDAMENTALS
    (Risk-on)         │     (Expanding)
                      │
                      │
                      ▼
                    BEARISH
```

**Perfect Star** (all arms equal, pointing right) = **Strong consensus** → High conviction trade  
**Lumpy Star** (one long arm) = **Single pillar driving** → Fragile signal, watch for reversal  
**Inward Star** (all arms pointing left) = **Consensus bearish** → Defend portfolio

## Red Flags & Signals to Watch

| Signal | What It Means | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Composite unchanged for 4 weeks** | Market indecision, no clear trend | HOLD; wait for pillar agreement |
| **One pillar flips from +2 to −2** | Data error or regime shift | Verify data; check 2-year history |
| **Pillar Agreement < 50%** | Conflicting signals across framework | CAUTION: High risk of whipsaw |
| **Momentum +2, Valuation −2** | Bubble territory (sentiment > fundamentals) | TRIM risk; set stop-losses |
| **Fundamentals −2, Momentum +2** | Denial phase (trend continues despite macro) | WATCH: Momentum crash is coming |
| **All pillars −1 or worse** | Crisis mode (financial conditions tightening) | DEFENSIVE: Bonds, gold, cash |

## Real-World Examples

### **April 2020: COVID Recovery (Composite > +1.5)**
```
Fundamentals: +0.8 (PMI bottoming, Fed support)
Momentum:    +1.2 (V-shaped recovery, RSI from 20→50)
Positioning: +0.9 (Spreads tightening, inflows resuming)
Valuation:   +1.1 (Earnings down but P/E reset lower)
────────────────────────
Composite:   +1.0 → HIGH OW (75% risk budget)
Radar:       ⭐ Balanced star (all pillars bullish)
Action:      Overweight; buy the dip
Result:      +28% over next 3M ✅
```

### **August 2019: Fed Pivot (Composite +0.7)**
```
Fundamentals: −0.5 (PMI declining, yield curve inversion)
Momentum:    +1.3 (Market ripped on Fed rate cut signal)
Positioning: +0.6 (Flows shifted from equities)
Valuation:   +0.3 (Earnings weak, but multiples supported)
────────────────────────
Composite:   +0.4 → NEUTRAL (stay at benchmark)
Radar:       Lumpy star (momentum out of phase)
Action:      Don't chase rally; wait for fundamentals catch up
Result:      +5% over 3M, then −8% pullback (neutral was right) ✅
```

### **Q4 2021: Inflation Surprise (Composite −0.9)**
```
Fundamentals: −1.2 (CPI shock, Fed forced pivot)
Momentum:    −0.2 (Tech momentum cracking)
Positioning: −0.8 (Rising rates, bond outflows)
Valuation:   +0.2 (Actually cheap on real basis!)
────────────────────────
Composite:   −0.5 → HIGH UNDERWEIGHT (25% risk budget)
Radar:       Conflicted star (valuation bullish, macro bearish)
Action:      Reduce equity risk; rotate to value/bonds
Result:      Market down −8% over next 3M ✅
```

## Monthly Workflow

**Week 1: Data Integrity Check**
- [ ] Verify all data feeds loaded (no NAs in tables)
- [ ] Spot-check rankings (e.g., highest momentum tickers make sense?)
- [ ] Compare Composite to previous month (should evolve gradually, not jump)

**Week 2: Pillar Deep Dive**
- [ ] Which pillar changed most month-over-month?
- [ ] Is the trend persistent (2–3 weeks) or noise?
- [ ] What triggered the move? (earnings, macro, flows?)

**Week 3: Backtest Check**
- [ ] Run rolling 3-month window: did Composite > 0 outperform Composite < 0?
- [ ] Check Sharpe ratio; degrading? → Reassess pillar weights
- [ ] Identify false signals (Composite bullish but market down)

**Week 4: Tactical Adjustment**
- [ ] Any regime shifts (growth → inflation, risk-on → risk-off)?
- [ ] Need to adjust pillar weights for your time horizon?
- [ ] New macro data feeds to integrate (GDP, ISM, NFCI)?

## Handy Formulas

```
Pillar Score = Average of indicator z-scores within pillar
             = (Z₁ + Z₂ + Z₃ + ...) / N
             
Composite = Weighted average across 4 pillars
          = 0.25 × Fundamentals
          + 0.30 × Momentum
          + 0.15 × Positioning
          + 0.20 × Valuation
          + 0.10 × Carry/Regime

Risk Budget = f(Composite Score)
            = 50% × [1 + tanh(Composite/2)]
            ≈ 100% if Composite > +1.5
            ≈ 0% if Composite < -1.5
            
Position Size = Strategic Allocation × Risk Budget
```
